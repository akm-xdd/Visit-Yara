Why visit Yara?

Hills

Yara is home to beautiful hills. Almendras Hills in Esperanza provides a great hiking place. It overlooks the great Oasis Plains and has resorts for your comfortable stay. The view is incredible and the experience is unforgettable.

Wildlife

Yara is blessed with a variety of animals. Yara has over 80 species of animals and all of them are stunningly beautiful. Yara also has wildlife shelters for abandoned animals. The Castillo National Zoo in Valle de Oro is one of our main attractions. Did you know? According to the folklore, there are 5 mythical animals roaming in far lands of Yara. Even though no one has been able to get a pictorial evidence of their existence, many people claim to have encountered these majestic beasts while some even claim to have hunted them.

Lakes and Rivers

Lakes are Yara's biggest flex. There are around a 100 lakes in Yara, each presenting its unique style. Higado Lake and Verde Lake are two largest lakes in Yara. Not only lakes, Yara has plenty of rivers as well. Nogal River, Sucio River, Sombra River and Verde River are some of the most beautiful rivers in Yara. These lakes and rivers provide an all out experience including boating, kayaking, diving and many more activities.

Beaches and Islands

Yara has hundreds of beaches and islands. These islands provide the best spots for your next adventure such as speedboating, paragliding, deep sea diving and many more. Also, their evening parties are badass!
